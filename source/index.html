A User’s Guide to The Periodic Table of Open Data

The elements that matter when providing increased access to data

    •

Leveraging our research on the variables that determine Open Data’s Impact, we have sought to categorize the elements that make a difference for open data initiatives across five categories—Problem and Demand Definition; Capacity and Culture; Governance and Standards; Personnel and Partnerships; and Risk Mitigation. (see here for full list)

The User’s Guide (PDF) is intended to empower data stewards and others seeking to increase effective access to data and can be used as a checklist and tool to weigh different elements according to their context and priorities. Filling in the forms (off/on-line) will enable you to take a more comprehensive and strategic view on what resources and interventions may be needed.
